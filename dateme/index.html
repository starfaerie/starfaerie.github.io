Optimism/Unconditional Love: I have an abundance mindset and natural proclivity for optimism. I can't help but believe that a world of abundance (both material and emotional) for all is both possible and realistic. I do not believe suffering is inevitable, instead I believe that we all deserve unconditional love from others and ourselves. I strive to eliminate coercion in all forms (including self-coercion) whenever possible. I think that trying to motivate and control people (including ourselves) with praise, rewards, guilt, shame, and punishments is a form of conditional love that crushes the naturally creative spirit within us. I believe people are good and well intentioned, that problems have solutions, and that all people are fallible, including and especially myself. I believe that I am the author of my own destiny and that the responsibility for my own experience in the world begins and ends with myself. I live my life in a constant state of awe and celebration of the intrinsic value of life and the limitless goodness in the world manifested in others by treating people (including myself, and children!) with compassion, humility, and unconditional love.
