Junebug, written April 2014

We're in the midst of winter
Clouds encroach upon the sun
She wastes away, I shiver,
glancing back before I run

Mortified, I'm mortified by what she has become
Fortified, I've fortified the way I say “I love”
You're only sixteen once
We made sixteen plus one
You're only sixteen once
We made sixteen more fun

Nostalgia
is my best friend
To think that's what I called her
But today we are /end
I was the April wind, and she was more than just a gale
Like colliding hurricanes we dissipated, still I'm

Mortified, I'm mortified by what we have become
Fortified, I've fortified the way I say “I love my
Junebug
I caught my Junebug
We met in June, bug
I called her my Junebug”

Are you Dead? written April 2014

I wish I could make peace tonight
I know I can't bring back the times
Your favourite place was by my side

But I'm trying through it all
'Cause your mind's my rise and fall
I know I can't go back in time
I try most nights and but stop for lack of perfect mind
So the memories fade on
But your heart just froze this song

Why the lack of closure?
Was I just a means to an end?
Why aren't we growing closer?
Did you really mean it when you said…

“A general lack of feelings, a false sense of healing,
these are the things I keep in your bed.”

Are you dead? 'Cause you seem dead to me.
I wish you were dead to me, but I can't seem to forget.

A time when talking wasn't a chore
We both just sat there, wanting each other more
A time for answers, but no obligations
Content with the world, and no hesitations

When did you get bored?
Did he bring something more?
So are you dead? You seem dead to me.
I wish you were dead to me, but I can't seem to forget.
